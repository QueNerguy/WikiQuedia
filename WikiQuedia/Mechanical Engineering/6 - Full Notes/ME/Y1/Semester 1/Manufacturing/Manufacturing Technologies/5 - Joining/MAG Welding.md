# MAG Welding
### Process
<u>M</u>etal <u>A</u>ctive <u>G</u>as welding is a welding process that uses a consumable elektrode to create plasma upon contact with the [workpiece](Manufacturing%20Technologies%20Hub#Terms%20and%20Disambiguation). This plasma melts the metal locally, allowing for great precision. The molten metal is mixed with the molten electrode to create a joint and fill in potential gaps. A coating is formed

### Characteristics
This process is for __metals__ only.
