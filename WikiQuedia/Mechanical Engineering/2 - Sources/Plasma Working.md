### Plasma Working
2025-01-29
#### Referencing:
[Wikipedia - Plasma cutting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plasma_cutting)

#### Synopsis:
Plasma working techniques generating plasma in a concentrated stream of gas. It works by blowing an electrically conductive cloud toward the [workpiece](Manufacturing%20Technologies%20Hub#Terms%20and%20Disambiguation) and running a current through it to the workpiece itself. This flow of electricity creates such heat the the gas turns to plasma and the metal is molten, to either seperate or join parts by plasma cutting or welding them respectively.
In order to use plasma to work on a product the product has to be able to cunduct electricity, therefore the material itself must be conducive but also the workbench which doubles as a ground for the electricity.
