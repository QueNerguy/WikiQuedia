#### Synopsis:
Torque, referred to in this document as $T$, measures an object's tendency to rotate. This would be the equivalent to a force, but in a rotational motion. Usually a distinguishment is made between torques and [moments](Moment.md) but physically they act the same on an object. A torque is a moment that __1:__ twists the object, or __2:__ a rotation - or rotational tendency - that is caused by _pure_ rotation.
