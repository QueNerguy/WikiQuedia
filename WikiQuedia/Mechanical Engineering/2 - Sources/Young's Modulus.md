### Young's Modulus
2025-01-27
#### Referencing:
[Wikipedia - Young's modulus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Young%27s_modulus)

#### Synopsis:
The Young's modulus ($E$) measures how much a material will elongate at a certain amount of stress. The higher the Young's modulus, the more stress a material can withstand while elongating the same amount, resulting in a high _stiffness_.

- $E$ increases: stiffness increases.
